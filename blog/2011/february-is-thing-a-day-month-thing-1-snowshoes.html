February is Thing-A-Day month! Thing #1: Snowshoes

February 01, 2011

February is thing-a-day month, and I'm participating.  Well, I'm not blogging with them, but I'm making something every day and blogging here about it. In honor, or perhaps in defiance, of the snowpocalypse setting in tonight, I made snowshoes.  It's just a rectangular wooden frame with a small platform to stand on and wrapped in fabric to add surface area.  It didn't work great, but it worked well enough.  You can see the effectiveness in the photoset linked below: [caption id=“” align=“aligncenter” width=“375” caption=“Snowshoe victory!”][/caption]
